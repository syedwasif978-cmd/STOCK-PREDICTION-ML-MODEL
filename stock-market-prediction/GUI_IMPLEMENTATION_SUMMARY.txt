MSE (Mean Squared Error):
  What: Average squared prediction error
  Lower: Better
  Example: 0.0023 is very good

RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error):
  What: Square root of MSE
  Interpretable in original units
  Example: 0.0484 = 4.84% average error

MAE (Mean Absolute Error):
  What: Average absolute error
  Example: 0.0318 = 3.18% typical error
  Most intuitive metric

RÂ² (R-squared):
  What: Proportion of variance explained
  Range: 0 to 1 (higher = better)
  
  Interpretation:
  0.90+  â†’ Excellent (90%+ variance explained)
  0.70+  â†’ Good (70%+ variance explained)
  0.50+  â†’ Moderate (reasonable predictions)
  0.30+  â†’ Poor (use with caution)
  <0.30  â†’ Very Poor (unreliable)
